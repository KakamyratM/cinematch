Our Mission

In 2026, we have 17,000+ streaming options… and still nobody to watch with.

Endless scrolling. Group chat ghosting. “What do you want to watch?” → silence.

Cinematch exists to end that loop.

We connect people who actually love the same movies, not just algorithms pushing content.
Then we give you the tools to turn “we have the same taste” into “let’s watch it together — tonight”.

What We’re Building

Deep movie taste matching

We go way beyond genres and ratings — directors, eras, vibes, rewatches, guilty pleasures, Letterboxd energy… we try to find your cinematic soulmate.

Frictionless watch parties

One-click synced viewing (coming soon), built-in chat, reactions, polls (“sub or dub?”, “intermission or no?”), and calendar invites.

A home for film people

Genre clubs, director watch-alongs, 90s kids nights, horror insomnia crews, Criterion stans, MCU haters — find your micro-community.

Founding Story

Cinematch started when three friends kept texting “anyone down to watch something tonight?”… and nobody ever replied.

We realized the problem wasn’t finding good movies — it was finding good people to watch them with.

So we decided to build the place we wished existed.

We’re still very early. Join now and help shape what movie nights look like for the next decade.
